Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

AMAZING Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Chicken Dip! This is a thick and creamy dip that tastes like buffalo wings, serve it up with some veggies or crackers!

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz package of softened cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Red Hot
  • 1/2 cup ranch or blue cheese sals dressing
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chciken
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • crackers, veggies, or chips for serving

Directions

  1. Heat oven 350 degrees
  2. Combine cream cheese, hot sauce, salad dressing, chicken, and shredded cheese in a 1 quart baking dish
  3. Bake 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through-out
  4. Garnish and serve with desired side
